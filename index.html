My Journey in Web Development: From Passion to Profession

My journey into web development began in 2013 when I wrote my first HTML code using the <marquee> tag. That simple tag sparked a passion for web design, leading me to explore the field through online resources and practice.

By 2014, I stepped into professional web development with WordPress and soon began freelancing, starting on Fiverr. My dedication and enthusiasm for creating functional, visually appealing websites helped me build a strong reputation.

In 2018, I faced a setback after losing my Fiverr account. However, this challenge became a turning point. After a short break, I returned stronger, rejoining Fiverr and expanding to platforms like Upwork and Fivesquid, taking on diverse projects that honed my skills further.

Over the years, I’ve adapted to the evolving web landscape and cultivated expertise in WordPress, React.js, Laravel, and Flutter. Today, I am passionate about delivering exceptional solutions and continuously innovating for my clients.
